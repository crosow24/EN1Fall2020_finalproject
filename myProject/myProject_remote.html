My Project (Remote Interface)

This project is a baseball based arcade game to be played over the web.

Your goal: strike out the automated batter by throwing strikes!

How to play: use the buttons below to increase or decrease the angle of the throwing arm. Then, chose the force of your throw.

Just hit stop between each action to make sure that the robot responds properly.
